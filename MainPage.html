Threads of Tradition

Experience the artistry of nito weaving, abaca fibers, and handcrafted pottery. Rooted in culture and refined through generations, these locally made products showcase both functionality and timeless design.

In Ibajay, weaving is more than a craft—it is a heritage passed down through generations. Using locally gathered nito vines and abaca fibers, artisans transform natural materials into functional and decorative pieces that reflect the community’s patience, skill, and deep connection to nature. Each weave carries the story of daily life shaped by tradition and resourcefulness.
